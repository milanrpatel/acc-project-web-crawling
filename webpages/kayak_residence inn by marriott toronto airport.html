The newly renovated Residence Inn Toronto Airport is an ideal hotel choice near Toronto Pearson Airport. Conveniently accessible to major highways 401, 427 which connects to highways Gardiner Expressway, QEW and 407. You’ll enjoy a new, modern lobby, connect with our free WiFi, and refuel with refreshments from The Market. Our spacious suites have been enhanced to include a new sectional sofa, ergonomic desk, updated bathrooms and contemporary color schemes which complement the suite’s separate spaces, along with a fully equipped kitchen. Start your day with our free hot breakfast featuring rotational, healthy and freshly made selections. At Residence Inn Toronto Airport we provide everything you need to maintain your balance and thrive. We can even do grocery shopping for you. An excellent hotel for extended stays!Marriott
