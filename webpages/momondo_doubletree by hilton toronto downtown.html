A landmark hotel in the heart of downtown Toronto located at 108 Chestnut St; Toronto in the city’s business hub. Conveniently located close to major Government Offices, Nathan Phillips Square, City Hall, World Class Hospitals, Universities, US Consulate & Diplomatic Missions, Toronto's Eaton Centre, Yonge/Dundas Square, TTC subway, underground PATH, major attractions and points of interest. The hotel features 490 modern non-smoking guest rooms including 34 suites. The standard guestrooms consist of the choice of standard king, queen, or two double beds. The 26 two-room suites boast kitchenettes and separate living area, are ideal for extended stays or guests looking for additional space. The hotel also has 4 Junior Suites which have an open-concept layout with kitchenettes as well as 4 Executive Suites, all of which are located on the 25th and 26th floors. The suites are large enough to accommodate intimate corporate meetings or receptions. The hotel offers a concierge service, uniformed security patrol, secure underground guest parking, and an ATM located in the lobby. Guests can stay connected with complimentary high-speed internet access throughout the hotel and 24 hour open-concept business centre. Maintain your exercise regime in the complimentary 24-hour Precor fitness center, unwind in the sauna or take a dip in the heated indoor swimming pool. Changing facilities are equipped with lockers. A laundry room is situated on the Mezzanine Level for guest convenience. Dine-in or takeout at our new concept on-site Hemisphere's Restaurant for breakfast, lunch or dinner. Relax and enjoy light fare and cocktails with friends or colleagues in our Bistro lounge. Room service is available for breakfast and dinner services. Travelers on the run can choose quality food or sundry items from our Made-Market off the lobby. Our delightful DoubleTree Cookies are readily available from our courteous and friendly guest service agents.Hilton
