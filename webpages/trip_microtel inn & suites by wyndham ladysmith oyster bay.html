Property Description

  • Opened: 2018
  • Number of Rooms: 81
With a stay at Microtel Inn & Suites by Wyndham Oyster Bay in North Oyster, you'll be within a 5-minute drive of Ladysmith Museum and Ladysmith Little Theatre. This eco-certified hotel is 12.1 mi (19.5 km) from Duke Point Ferry Terminal and 1.7 mi (2.8 km) from Ladysmith Marina.

Don't miss out on the many recreational opportunities, including an indoor pool, a waterslide, and a spa tub. Additional amenities at this hotel include complimentary wireless internet access, gift shops/newsstands, and a television in a common area.

A complimentary continental breakfast is served on weekdays from 6:00 AM to 9:00 AM and on weekends from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM.

Featured amenities include a business center, express check-out, and complimentary newspapers in the lobby. Planning an event in North Oyster? This hotel has 975 square feet (91 square meters) of space consisting of conference space and a meeting room. Free self parking is available onsite.

Make yourself at home in one of the 81 guestrooms featuring refrigerators and flat-screen televisions. Complimentary wireless internet access is available to keep you connected. Private bathrooms with bathtubs or showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include desks and microwaves, and housekeeping is provided daily.
