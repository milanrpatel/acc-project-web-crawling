Welcome to The Drake Hotel. We are a Toronto hotel destination rooted in contemporary art, culture, genuine hospitality and proudly celebrate our local surroundings along with the communities we serve. Since opening our doors in 2004, we have sought to create a neighbourhood hub that provides a unique sense of place; to bridge the art of the moment with intentional hospitality, to ignite curiosity within travellers, revellers, neighbours, and our local friends. From live music in the Underground to award-winning cocktails on our climate-defiant, year-round Sky Yard roof-top patio; from ongoing art exhibits and eats, there's always something happening at our West Queen West outpost. We invite you to experience our special blend of bespoke hotel and inclusive hub for unique and diverse experiences with great seasonal menus, a robust cocktail list, a vibrant local culture, carefully curated curiosities, and genuine hospitality.iHotelier/iStay
