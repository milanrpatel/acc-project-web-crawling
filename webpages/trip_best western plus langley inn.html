Property Description

  • Renovated: 2012
  • Number of Rooms: 77
A stay at Best Western Plus Langley Inn places you in the heart of Langley, within a 5-minute drive of The Great Escape and Cascades Casino. This golf hotel is 1.4 mi (2.2 km) from Willowbrook Shopping Centre and 1.4 mi (2.3 km) from Milner Downs Equestrian Center.

Take advantage of recreational opportunities offered, including an indoor pool, a spa tub, and a fitness center. This hotel also features complimentary wireless internet access, a fireplace in the lobby, and a vending machine.

Enjoy a satisfying meal at abc restaurant and lounge serving guests of Best Western Plus Langley Inn. A complimentary on-the-go breakfast is included.

Featured amenities include a 24-hour business center, dry cleaning/laundry services, and a 24-hour front desk. Free self parking is available onsite.

Make yourself at home in one of the 79 guestrooms featuring refrigerators and LCD televisions. Complimentary wired and wireless internet access keeps you connected, and cable programming provides entertainment. Private bathrooms have designer toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include desks and coffee/tea makers, and housekeeping is provided daily.
