Property Description

  • Opened: 2020
  • Renovated: 2020
  • Number of Rooms: 35
Located in Revelstoke, Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Revelstoke is in the mountains, within a 10-minute drive of Revelstoke Railway Museum and Columbia River. This hotel is 7.7 mi (12.4 km) from Revelstoke Mountain Resort and 3.3 mi (5.3 km) from Revelstoke Golf Club.

This smoke-free hotel features snowboarding nearby, ecotours nearby, and sledding nearby. Recreational activities include rock climbing nearby, hot springs nearby, and downhill skiing nearby.

A complimentary self-serve breakfast is served on weekdays from 6:30 AM to 9:30 AM.

Featured amenities include complimentary wired internet access, a 24-hour business center, and complimentary newspapers in the lobby. Free self parking is available onsite.

Stay in one of 87 guestrooms featuring LCD televisions. Complimentary wired and wireless internet access keeps you connected, and satellite programming provides entertainment. Private bathrooms with bathtubs or showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include desks and irons/ironing boards, as well as phones with free local calls.
