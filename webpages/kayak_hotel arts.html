Offering an outdoor pool and bar with a retractable roof and a fitness centre, Hotel Arts is located in Calgary city centre. Free WiFi access is available throughout the property. Calgary Stampede is just 1 km away. All rooms offer a cable TV, in-room safe, and Keurig coffee machine. Private bathrooms also come with Skoah bath amenities and a bath. A workstation with power outlets and USB ports, and an iron and ironing board are provided. Select rooms offer a city or pool view. At Calgary Hotel Arts you will find the on-site restaurant Yellow Door Bistro and Raw Bar. Other facilities offered include spa services, luggage storage, and complimentary bike rentals. Underground self parking and valet parking is available. Calgary Zoo and various shops are within 3 km from this hotel. CORE Shopping Centre is 3 minutes’ drive away.Booking.com

6.0 OkayJaimin, Nov. 2023

Hotel was nice and staff were friendly. The room was spacious and the balcony had a nice view even though we were only on the 4th / 5th floor. It was quite quiet at night and we did get a good night sleep. The bed was huge and a really good size! Great location in downtown Calgary and a short walk to Calgary tower.

The main purpose of our stay here was to use the pool facilities at the end of our 3-week road trip and we have paid extra for early check in just to use the pool. When we checked in around 1pm, we asked if we could book the pool, but reception staff advised that the pool was out of action for the rest of the day due to maintenance and we couldn't use it until later that evening. I think they should have told us when we were arranging the early check in that we couldn't use the main facility of the hotel! We would have stayed elsewhere at a cheaper rate if we knew we couldn't use the pool that day. The room was okay, but the bathroom was dated and seemed no better than a holiday inn / Travelodge.

Bed was comfy. Nice view of the Calgary Tower. Nice big windows. Good desk (a little in the way of getting around). Nice to have 2 eating and drinking options in the hotel. Swimming pool was being fixed so couldn't use it at that time, but that would have been good. Gym on site.

Bath products (shampoo, moisturiser, body wash) provided were very "refreshing" to the point of making my eyes and "other" parts sting!! The sink was in the main room - so not very hygienic as you have to exit the toilet to wash your hands. Toilet roll holder too far from the seat! Some staff were amazing. Some very indifferent.
