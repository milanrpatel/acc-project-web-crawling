Nestled on 15 acres of scenic parkland, and a 16-minute drive from the city centre of Toronto, this hotel features a seasonal outdoor pool. Each of the rooms are tastefully decorated and air conditioned. An iron is also included. Toiletries and a hairdryer can be found in the bathroom. Guests at the Pan Pacific Toronto can also enjoy a variety of on-site activities, including tennis courts. The hotel also features several on-site restaurants offering a variety of international cuisines, as well as a state-of-the-art fitness centre. Pan Pacific Toronto is conveniently located close to several major highways. This location allows guests to easily visit the sites of the downtown area, including the CN Tower, as well as other area attractions. Canada's Wonderland amusement park is within 21 km from the property.Booking.com
